Welcome · Introduction

❧

中華文化，源遠流長，博大精深。自三皇五帝以降，歷夏商周秦漢唐宋元明清，數千年文明傳承， 凝聚了無數哲人的智慧與藝術的精華，為人類文明留下了不朽的遺產。

Chinese civilization stretches back thousands of years, accumulating an unparalleled treasury of philosophy, literature, and art. From the legendary sage-kings through the great dynasties, its thinkers and poets have left an immortal legacy for all of humanity.
