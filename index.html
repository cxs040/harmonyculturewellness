Confucianism

❧

"學而時習之，不亦說乎？有朋自遠方來，不亦樂乎？"

"Is it not pleasant to learn with constant perseverance? Is it not delightful to have friends coming from distant quarters?"

— 《論語·學而》
— Analects of Confucius, Book I

五常：核心德目

The Five Constants

仁
仁 · 仁愛 Rén · Benevolence

愛人，人與人之間相互敬愛的道德情感

Love and compassion for others; the highest moral virtue

義
義 · 正義 Yì · Righteousness

行事合乎道德規範，以正義為準則

Acting in accordance with moral principles and justice

禮
禮 · 禮儀 Lǐ · Ritual Propriety

禮儀規範，是社會秩序與和諧的基礎

Rites and rituals that form the foundation of harmonious society

智
智 · 智慧 Zhì · Wisdom

明辨是非，擁有道德判斷力與洞察力

Discerning right from wrong; moral wisdom and insight

信
信 · 誠信 Xìn · Integrity

言而有信，誠實守諾，立身之本

Faithfulness, trustworthiness, and keeping one's word

道家思想

Taoism

❧

"道可道，非常道；名可名，非常名。無名天地之始，有名萬物之母。"

"The Tao that can be told is not the eternal Tao; the name that can be named is not the eternal name. The nameless is the origin of heaven and earth; the named is the mother of ten thousand things."

— 《道德經》第一章 · 老子
— Tao Te Ching, Chapter I · Laozi

核心概念

Core Concepts

道
道 · 宇宙本源 Tào · The Way

萬物之本，宇宙運行的根本規律，不可言說，只可體悟

The fundamental principle underlying all existence; the natural order of the universe beyond words

德
德 · 內在德性 Dé · Virtue & Power

順道而行所積累的內在力量與品德修為

The power and virtue accumulated through living in harmony with the Tao

無
無為 · 自然而然 Wúwéi · Non-Action

順應自然，不強行妄為；以柔克剛，以靜制動

Acting in harmony with the natural flow; yielding and softness overcome the forceful and rigid

樸
樸 · 純樸自然 Pǔ · Simplicity

返璞歸真，摒棄人為的繁瑣，回歸本真

Returning to simplicity; shedding artifice to rediscover one's authentic nature

靜夜思 Thoughts on a Quiet Night
李白 · 唐 Li Bai · Tang Dynasty
床前明月光，
疑是地上霜。
舉頭望明月，
低頭思故鄉。
Before my bed the moonlight gleams,
I wonder if it is frost on the ground.
Raising my head I gaze at the bright moon,
Bowing my head I think of my distant home.
春望 Spring View
杜甫 · 唐 Du Fu · Tang Dynasty
國破山河在，
城春草木深。
感時花濺淚，
恨別鳥驚心。
The nation is broken; mountains and rivers remain.
Spring comes to the city; grass and trees grow deep.
Moved by the times, flowers draw tears;
Grieving separation, birds startle the heart.
水調歌頭 Prelude to Water Melody
蘇軾 · 宋 Su Shi · Song Dynasty
明月幾時有，把酒問青天。
不知天上宮闕，今夕是何年。
人有悲歡離合，月有陰晴圓缺，
此事古難全。但願人長久，千里共嬋娟。
How long has the bright moon been there?
I raise my cup to ask the blue sky.
People have joys and sorrows, partings and reunions;
The moon waxes and wanes — this has ever been so.
I only wish for enduring life, to share this moonlight across a thousand miles.
登鸛雀樓 Ascending Stork Tower
王之渙 · 唐 Wang Zhihuan · Tang Dynasty
白日依山盡，
黃河入海流。
欲窮千里目，
更上一層樓。
The white sun descends behind the mountains;
The Yellow River flows into the sea.
To exhaust the view a thousand miles distant,
Ascend yet one more storey of the tower.
